# Common cold

General information only. Not medical advice.

## What it is

The common cold is a viral infection of the upper respiratory tract. Many viruses can cause cold-like symptoms; rhinoviruses are among the most common.

## Typical symptoms

- Runny or stuffy nose
- Sneezing
- Sore throat
- Mild cough
- Sometimes mild fever or fatigue

## When to see a doctor

See a healthcare provider if symptoms worsen, last longer than about 10 days, or you develop high fever, difficulty breathing, or severe throat pain. This is general guidance; always consult a qualified professional for your situation.
